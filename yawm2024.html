Yunnan Normal University Mathematics Conference 2025

August 4–14, 2025

Department of Mathematics, Yunnan Normal University, Kunming, China

About the Conference

This conference aims to bring together researchers in various fields of mathematics to foster collaboration and exchange of ideas. Topics include but are not limited to representation theory, differential geometry, harmonic analysis, PDEs, number theory, and mathematical physics.

Scientific Program

The program will consist of invited talks, contributed talks, and informal discussions. The detailed schedule will be posted closer to the conference dates.
